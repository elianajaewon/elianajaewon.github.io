Intro

Welcome to my page!

Thanks for taking an interest in my journey. I love to build and create things, which is probably why I went from being a musician to a recruiter and now a developer.

I completed a three month intensive coding bootcamp through Actualize Coding Bootcamp & Apprenticeship where I learned the skills to be a software developer. I learned how to program in Ruby, Rails, Javascript, VueJS, and more. I also learned how to think like a developer in order to problem solve and tackle all types of technology challenges.
